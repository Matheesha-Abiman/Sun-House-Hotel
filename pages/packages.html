Our Packages


Honeymoon Package


Romantic getaway with exclusive amenities.
The Honeymoon Package at The Sun House is designed for couples seeking an unforgettable romantic escape. Guests are welcomed into a luxurious suite adorned with soft romantic décor and a private balcony overlooking the scenic beauty of Galle.
A chilled bottle of champagne and a bouquet of fresh flowers await your arrival, setting the mood for your intimate getaway. Enjoy a candle-lit dinner under the stars, with a specially curated menu that speaks the language of love.
Rejuvenate together with a couples' spa session that includes soothing massages and fragrant oils. Each morning begins with breakfast served in the comfort of your room, and evenings are made extra special with a romantic turndown service featuring rose petals. To make your stay even more relaxed, we offer late checkout upon request, allowing you to soak in every moment of your romantic retreat.

Family Package


Perfect for a family vacation.
Our Family Package is perfect for creating cherished memories with your loved ones in a comfortable and entertaining environment.
Families are accommodated in spacious suites that include extra beds or connecting rooms to ensure both comfort and convenience.
On arrival, children are delighted with welcome goodie bags filled with fun treats. Mornings begin with a hearty breakfast buffet for all family members, and days are filled with exciting adventures such as guided tours around Galle, visits to nearby beaches, and engaging indoor activities.
Parents can enjoy a quiet evening out, thanks to our complimentary babysitting service.
In the evenings, the family can come together for movie nights or poolside fun, all while enjoying discounts on meals and transport services. It’s a worry-free getaway that caters to every member of the family.

Adventure Package


Thrilling experiences for adventure seekers.
For those who crave excitement and discovery, the Adventure Package offers a thrilling blend of nature and adrenaline. Guests stay in eco-style suites perched on the hillside, offering panoramic views and close proximity to adventure trails.
Each day begins with a carefully planned itinerary that might include rainforest treks, wildlife safaris, snorkeling in crystal-clear waters, or whale watching along the southern coast. For land explorers, cycling tours through Galle’s colonial forts and coastal paths offer both cultural insights and physical thrills. Packed lunches and snacks are prepared for your journeys, and experienced guides ensure every adventure is safe and memorable.
After an action-filled day, unwind with a session of yoga at sunrise or a calming massage in the evening, balancing energy with serenity. This package is perfect for explorers who want to dive into the spirit of adventure without compromising on comfort.

Wellness Package


The Wellness Package is crafted for guests looking to unwind, recharge, and reconnect with themselves in a peaceful and nurturing environment. From the moment you arrive, you’ll be embraced by the calm ambiance of nature and tranquility. Each morning starts with a guided sunrise yoga session overlooking the lush garden surroundings, followed by a nutritious, detox-friendly breakfast.

Culinary Experience Package


The Culinary Experience Package is a gourmet journey through the flavors and spices of Sri Lanka.

Ideal for food lovers and curious palates, this package includes exclusive access to cooking classes with our in-house chefs, where guests can learn traditional Sri Lankan recipes using fresh, local ingredients. Enjoy curated dining experiences each evening, including multi-course meals that highlight regional specialties and unique fusion dishes. A private spice garden tour and a visit to the local market provide insights into the ingredients and stories behind each dish.
Each guest receives a custom recipe book and a spice sampler to take a piece of the culinary adventure home.It’s more than a stay—it’s a flavorful memory.
