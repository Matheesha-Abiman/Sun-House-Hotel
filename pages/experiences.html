AT THE SUN HOUSE

Arrack Tasting

Immerse yourself in a captivating arrack tasting experience that celebrates the rich flavors of Sri Lanka’s iconic coconut spirit. Enjoy a carefully curated selection of signature cocktails, each showcasing the versatility of arrack while incorporating local ingredients and innovative techniques. These exquisite cocktails are perfectly paired with a series of local Sri Lankan street food, reimagined with a refined twist to elevate your dining experience. 12 hours notice required.

Massages

Indulge in personalized massage treatments designed to soothe both body and mind. Our expert therapists use a blend of traditional techniques and modern wellness practices, tailored to meet your specific needs. Whether you’re seeking deep tissue relief, or a rejuvenating Ayurvedic massage, each experience is crafted to promote relaxation and restore balance.

Cooking Classes

Join our Sun House Sri Lankan cooking class for a hands-on experience. Get ingredients from the local market and learn to cook authentic dishes, suitable for all skill levels. It's an educational and enjoyable journey into the heart of Sri Lankan cuisine.

GALLE FORT EXCURSION

Culture Walk

Discover the UNESCO heritage site with our host, a charming and quirky host featured in BBC, Condé Nast Traveler, and various other magazines. With his friendly approach, they will guide you through the town’s rich colonial history, its vibrant modern culture, and the stories behind some of the area’s top attractions. Expect an experience filled with fascinating insights, laughter, and lasting memories! Meanwhile, children aged 3 to 10 can enjoy a picture-based treasure hunt led by local guide, ensuring fun and engagement for the whole family.

Cycling Tours

Cycle around Galle Fort with a knowledgeable local guide, experiencing the town's charm firsthand. This tour takes you to must-see highlights of the UNESCO heritage site, showcasing its rich history and vibrant culture. And don’t forget to pause for a refreshing ice cream break along the way—it’s a must!

OTHER ACTIVITIES

Unawatuna Cycling Tour

Hop on a bicycle and explore the coastal village of Unawatuna, the birthplace of tourism after the Tsunami. With a local guide leading the way, enjoy a thrilling off-road ride through the village, past marshlands and a serene lake teeming with wildlife, including fishing cats and eagles. Along the journey, visit a traditional rope factory and take a tour of a clothing factory. Refresh yourself the local way with a fresh coconut in hand as you soak in the vibrant sights and culture.

Surf Lessons

Learn to surf with some of the best ISA certified instructors in the south coast. They will make your surfing journey fun and safe by guiding you to the waters, and helping you catch your waves. For the experienced surfers, they will take you to the advanced surf spots to get you back to riding the fun waves that Sri Lanka offers.

Whale Watching Trip

Sail the picturesque south coast waters and spot the largest mammals to have ever existed on the planet, the mighty blue whale. The luxurious catamaran is set to deliver the best sailing experience in the region by offering you the chance to spot different species of dolphins, Orcas, and other whales as well. Pack light with some sunscreen and a towel, as the meals are provided, and you will have a chance to swim and paddleboard the beautiful waters in the south coast.

Sunset Catamaran Sail

A perfect way to end the evening with your loved ones. The three hour sailing experience will showcase the colours of the tropical sky close to the equator, while you are provided with refreshments, and a chance to take on board a bottle of wine or champagne of your choice.

Handunugoda Tea Estate

Discover the timeless craft of tea production with a visit to a captivating lowland tea plantation in Ahangama. Stroll through the estate and tour their charming on-site tea factory, where you'll learn about the process behind their prized tea variety, renowned and served in famous tea houses worldwide. Enjoy a stop in the tasting room for a cup of oolong tea paired with cake. On your way, don't miss the opportunity to witness the stunning sight of traditional stilt fishing.

Diving, Jet Skiing and Snorkeling

Adventures in the deep sea with certified instructors guiding you to experience the underwater life that Sri Lanka has to offer. Get certified with the diving course, or just snorkel/dive for the day exploring shipwrecks while having your photos taken. You can also go on fishing tours, and enjoy your day by the beach riding the Jet Ski in the bay of Unawatuna.

Jungle Yoga

Step into the jungle and immerse yourself in movement and mindfulness at a serene yoga shala, surrounded by lush greenery. Led by an experienced wellness instructor, each session offers a perfect blend of yoga and nature. After your practice, take a refreshing dip in the pool to cool off. Be sure to ask us for the daily schedule!

DAY TOURS

Sinharaja Rainforest

Embark on a day trip to one of Sri Lanka’s most renowned rainforests, a lush green paradise teeming with birdlife and biodiversity. As a UNESCO World Heritage site, this rainforest is home to over 95% of the country’s endemic birds and about 60% of its endemic trees, making it a photographer’s dream.

Yala Safari

Visit the national park with the highest leopard density in the world! Home to Sri Lankan leopards, elephants, sloth bears, deer, mugger crocodiles, langurs, wild buffalos, eagles, and various birds/animals in and around the tropical island. The guided tour will make sure that you have experience the best of the wild on your holiday here.

Customized Day Trips

Inquire our front desk to help us plan your day trips around the region, whether it is to see architectural marvels, visit the best beaches on the coast or to go on your own little adventure. We will help organise your trip.
